Meet the brains trust behind Hunter Token

Hunter Token is a visionary product of our founder and CEO, Chris Watkins. Through strategic foresight, drive, and determination, Chris built a strong foundation for his successful multimillion-dollar company TSA Outdoors, and followed it up with his very own ZeroTech Optics brand of firearms, which has quickly become a major player in the American and Australian markets with partners globally in the distribution of hunting gear.

This foresight is what led him to develop Digital Arms and Hunter Token ($HNTR) in 2021. Chris has taken his savvy negotiation and high-performance team building skills to pave the way for a digital ecosystem that aims to take hunting and gaming enthusiasts into the digital future, having visualized a market with great prospects for growth.
