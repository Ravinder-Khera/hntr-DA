What is Hunter Token?
As the buzz around NFTs rises, and the market for the buying and selling of digital assets with cryptocurrency wallets steadily grows, we identified a gap in the lack of a platform for the purchase, ownership and trade of digital firearms and hunting accessories. This gap was seized as an opportunity, and the idea of Hunter Token was born.
Hunter Token ($HNTR) is a cryptocurrency token built on the Binance Smart Chain (BEP20) to be used throughout Digital Arms, the world’s first platform for the buying and selling of firearm NFTs and digital accessories. It will also enable merchants to sell and promote their digital assets using $HNTR as payment and for discreet advertisements. Game developers can use $HNTR to facilitate players’ use of their own firearm NFTs through in-game transactions.
We will take full advantage of the NFTs created on our Digital Arms ecosystem, and maximize their utility so our users are always guaranteed to purchase valuable assets.
Through the Digital Armory, users can browse through the selection of NFT firearms and accessories available on sale, with selections available by brand, model and color. It includes:
  • the purchase of custom cosmetic options for your NFTs, or digital skins (Dskins);
  • gamification features to optimize your NFT for use around the metaverse; and
  • upgrades and accessories for your NFT firearms.
Users can also trade NFTs between themselves on this platform.
Merchants who wish to promote and sell their physical firearms and hunting accessories as digital assets can do so through our dedicated Digital Arms eCommerce platform, which will then be marketed to our wide network of enthusiasts through the Digital Armory.

HIGHEST QUALITY NFT FIREARMS WITH PINPOINT ACCURACY

Through our partnerships with leading firearms brands worldwide, we aim to create the world’s highest quality, exclusive NFT firearms.

Our Digital Arms NFTs will be built from the actual computer-aided-design (CAD) used to manufacture the firearm, ensuring pinpoint accuracy and precision in their digital recreations.

Each Digital Arms NFT will belong to one of several rarity classes, making them scarce, highly collectible and sought after.These digital firearms can be maximized to their full utility, guaranteeing our users valuable assets with every purchase. The Digital Arms ecosystem also offers high quality NFT firearms accessories that can be equipped to existing digital weapons, including optical sights, grips, high-capacity magazines, and suppressors.

With the unique identifier belonging to each NFT, these digital firearms can then be recreated within other ecosystems, such as video games, first-person shooter games, and other digital environments in the metaverse.
